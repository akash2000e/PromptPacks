Our story

Built from
experience

Prompt Packs was founded on 17 August 2016 by a team with deep roots in the packaging industry. Our partners had spent years working in this field — both in Kerala and abroad — and brought that hands-on experience together to build something of their own. What started as a shared vision has grown into a trusted manufacturer serving businesses pan-India, with a team of skilled and experienced staff and the capacity to produce 7,000 boxes every single day.
